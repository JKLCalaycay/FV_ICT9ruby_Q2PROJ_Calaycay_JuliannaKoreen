Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with a creamy sauce of eggs, Parmesan or Pecorino cheese, pancetta or guanciale, and black pepper. The hot pasta is tossed with the sauce, which creates a rich, velvety coating. It's a quick, comforting dish typically served with spaghetti.

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, spiced meat (like chicken or beef), and a blend of fragrant spices such as cumin, coriander, and cardamom. It's cooked in layers, often garnished with fried onions, fresh herbs, and served with yogurt or a side salad for added richness.
